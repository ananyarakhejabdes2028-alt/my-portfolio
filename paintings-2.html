TABULA RASA

Material Used: Pencil, acrylic paint on paper

Size: 14x20

Year: 2023

As I face the blank canvas, a mix of trepidation and excitement surge through me. Its emptiness is daunting and I grapple to fill it. Amidst my uncertainty, I fail to realize that I, myself, am a work of art in progress. With each stroke, I not only explore the canvas but also discover the depths of my own self.
